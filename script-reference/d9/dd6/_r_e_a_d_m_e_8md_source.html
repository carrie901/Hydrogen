20 Installation
21 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
22 
23 If you are cloning this repository you will more then likely need to copy it into the working

35 Folders
36 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
37 
38 ### Components ###
39 Here is where you find some pre-made drag and drop components to drop on to your GameObjects, or
40 you can add them by the quick add button. We've prefixed all of our component based scripts with
41 'h' so that they can be easily found and identified.
42 
43 ### Editors ###
44 Some fun swag for the Unity Editor, nothing major though.
45 
46 ### Extras ###
47 This is kind of a storage folder for things that our scripts will utilize automatically for you.
48 We would advise not playing with this folder, hell any folders really, that much.
49 
50 ### Plugins ###
51 This is where bulk of the code is kept. It is isolated in its own namespace as to not interfere
52 with anything else.
53 
54 ### Shaders ###
55 Pretty self explanatory don't you think?
56 
57 ### Vendors ###
58 If we've included something to make your life easier that we didn't particularly make or contribute
59 to; this will be its home.
